About Our School

Barrackpore Cantonment Vidyapith is one of the oldest Hindi medium schools in Barrackpore, Kolkata, teaching grades from V to XII under the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education and the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education. It is a Higher Secondary School with Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. This school has been dedicated to providing quality education for over 50 years, focusing on academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and holistic development of students.
